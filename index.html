About The Synexzia Technical Symposium

SYNEXZIA 2K25 is the flagship technical symposium organized by the Department of Information Technology. Our vision is to provide a forum where technology meets innovation and knowledge comes to provide solutions to real-world problems. Join us for an exciting day of learning, competition, and networking.

WHERE

At the Agni College of Technology,Department Of Information Technology

WHEN

Saturday, 5th April 2025
